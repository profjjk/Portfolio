My Crazy Journey

Ever since I was a little boy I dreamed of being a web developer… and if you believe that, then I’ve got a bridge to sell you. In truth, my path to becoming a web developer has been a long and unpredictable journey. I was a psychologist who dreamt of being a pilot, a wunderlust-stricken English teacher, an insufficiently greedy investment advisor, and an automotive entrepreneur who thinks sinking money into cars is wasteful. It’s been quite the journey.

For me, life hasn’t been a carefully orchestrated symphony of coordinated harmonies telling a single coherent story. My life’s soundtrack is more akin to jazz: largely improvised and full of off-beat rhythms and deliberate deviations in pitch. I seek to harness the chaotic beats of life into something new and beautiful. It's that ability to improvise and discover interesting ideas that fuels my work as a web developer. I bring the full weight of my insatiable curiosity, globe-trotting experience, and multifaceted professionalism to bear in creating delightful and functional websites that are sure to please.
